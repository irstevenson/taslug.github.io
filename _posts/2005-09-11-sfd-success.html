Hi all,

Well SFD is now over until next year and we had an extremely successful day!

I was interviewed on ABC Local radio who had dedicated their entire computer talkback to Free software and they had a tremendous response from their listeners (in fact, the show went overtime to accommodate them all!).

The displays at the Glenorchy Library were also a tremendous success with at least 50 people attending throughout the day - many coming along after hearing the radio talkback. We got lots of positive response from the public who were very interested in Free software and Linux.
