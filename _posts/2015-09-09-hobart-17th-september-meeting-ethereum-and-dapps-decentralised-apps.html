Hi LUGgers,

This month I am going to report on my journey down the rabbit hole of distributed systems and Ethereum. It has been a while since I was keenly interested in Bitcoin, but there have been some recent developments in block chain technology that have caught my eye. Specifically, Ethereum, a turing complete distributed VM where you can run your apps completely decentralised. Have your code executed in a secure and verifiable manner since it is validated on the block chain. Ethereum is still in early beta so it is a bit rough but I've managed to make a bit of headway into this new tech.
