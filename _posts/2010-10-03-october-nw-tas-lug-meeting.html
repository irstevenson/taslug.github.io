At the October meeting, Izak Newton demonstrated VLC media player on a netbook running the new Aptosid Linux, while Bob Mesibov talked about Australian suppliers of computers with Linux pre-installed.

The NW Tasmanian Linux Users Group meets on the first Saturday of every month at the Seabrook Hotel in Somerset. Next meeting 6 November, 1 pm for lunch and 2 pm for presentations.

Meetings are open to present and future Linux users, and intending users can bring along a laptop or netbook for a free trial installation and advice from the group.
