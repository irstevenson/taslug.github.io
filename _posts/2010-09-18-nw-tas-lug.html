The NW Tasmanian Linux User Group is meeting on the first Saturday of every month at the Seabrook Hotel, Bass Highway, Somerset. Come at 1 pm for lunch and chat, and/or 2 pm for the meeting. Next meeting is 2 October, when Isaac will demo he VLC media player and Bob will show that you can buy computers with Linux pre-installed in Australia!
