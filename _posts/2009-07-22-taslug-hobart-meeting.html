TasLUG Hobart Meeting.

Hello All,

It's been quite a few months since TasLUG has met in Hobart but after
linux.conf.au 2009 in Tasmania it's about time to stir up that local
community again! So come along, meet your local Linux community and
discuss all that open source goodness.

Date: 04/08/09

Time: 6:30 dinner, 7:30 start.
Please join us for an informal dinner at 6:30 beforehand in the restaurant area,
but feel free to come by at 7:30 as well.

Location: Hotel Soho
124 Davey Street, South Hobart 7000
(Corner of Davey and Molle St.)

To get us warmed up again, Leah Duncan will be doing a talk on what Linux Australia
is all about. She will also give us an overview of the background
and history of TasLUG and the Linux community in general. Leah is Linux
Australia's current secretary and also one of the chief organisers of the recent linux.conf.au.

After the formal talk we hope to open the floor to the community to discuss the
future of TasLUG. Things such as the format of meetings, content for the
presentations and perhaps even the date+venue.

For those who are keen, we will likely stay on after the formal part of the meeting for
drinks, informal discussions and socialising. So make sure you hang around and
chil out with your fellow TasLUGers!

If you are unable to make this meeting please send us an email or leave a comment as we'd
love to hear from you. Currently we are aiming for bi-monthly meetings on the first Tuesday
of each month. However, if there are enough people preferring a different date then that
could be considered.

Hope to see you all there!

Cheers,
