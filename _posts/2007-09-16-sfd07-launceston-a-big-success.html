Wow, what a day. After a slow start and a few monitor failures (thankfully we had spares), final estimates put us at somewhere between 80 and 100 people.

We were interviewed by a nice young lass from ABC radio named Siobhan, who is planning on airing a story on the event at some stage within the next few days.

We had 26 survey forms filled out and a huge amount of direct positive feedback during the event.

A big thanks to Leah and Joel for their mention on the radio this morning, and an extra huge thank-you to everyone who showed up. There was a real sense of community, and the genuine interest - both from those eager to learn something new and from those who came for the company of fellow FOSS enthusiasts certainly made the last two hectic weeks worthwhile.

More after we've had some sleep.
