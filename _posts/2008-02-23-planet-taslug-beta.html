TasLUG has now entered the world of blogging… we're trialling a Planet feed for the next few weeks, so if you have a blog and you think it would be of interest to our community, feel free to submit it.

We expect everyone to use discretion about what is appropriate to blog here; the feed will be accessible to the general public so keep the audience in mind. Keep it civil, legal and interesting!
