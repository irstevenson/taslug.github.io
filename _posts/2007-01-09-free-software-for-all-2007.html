G'day everyone

As a follow up to our Software Freedom Day event in Launceston last year, we are planning to hold another event (which we're titling "Free Software For All") on Sunday the 25th of February.


We will be covering 5 major areas:
* Home productivity - Showing off OpenOffice, Firefox, the usual stuff.
* Business productivity - We're hoping to demo an open source POS setup with a wedge bar code scanner. Business productivity will also cover 'specialist' apps like Blender3D, Eclipse and Audacity.
* Multi-media - Audio/Video playing, streaming, transcoding, etc.
* Distros/WMs - Helping newbies understand the giant confusing mass of operating system and window manager variants.
* Games - Neverball, Nexuiz, Nethack, Armagetron, Scorched3D, etc. We'll be also having some friendly tournaments (with prizes if all goes well).


It's going to be a fantastic day. Anyone who wants to show up is of course, more than welcome.


Anyone that's interested in assisting with the planning or has some resources they'd like to donate, should shoot an email off to Waide at waide.soper@gmail.com


We'll post an update once we've confirmed the event time.
