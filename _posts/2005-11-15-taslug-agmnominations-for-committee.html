Hi All,

It's that time of year again where we have our AGM, committee nominations and elections. The AGM will be held on Tuesday the 29th on irc.taslug.scriptforge.org #taslug 7:30 PM. Nominations are open as of today 14/11/05 and close on the 22/11/05 (5pm).

Read on for more details:
AGM</strong>
The AGM will be held on Tuesday the 29th on irc.taslug.scriptforge.org #taslug 7:30 PM. An agenda will be made available one week before the meeting. If anyone wants to get anything put on the agenda please send it to me or Leah before 5pm on the 21st November.

Nominations for Committee</strong>
All nominations for committee must be received no later than 5pm 22nd of November. You can either nominate by posting to the mailing list (prob. the best way) or by emailing/PM'ing the nomination to Gary (TasLUG's public officer) or Ben. Nominations need not be seconded, you can nominate for more than one position and you can nominate yourself. Nominations will only be taken at the AGM for positions that are not already filled - so nominate beforehand!
The positions are:
President
Vice-President
Treasurer
Secretary

Voting</strong>
If more than one person nominates for a position a ballot will be held. Voting will be conducted via email over the week before the AGM.
