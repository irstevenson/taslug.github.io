Hi all,

Software Freedom Day is just around the corner, and preparations are in full swing! It's on Saturday 15th of September, and will be held at the Wesley Church Hall in Melville St, Hobart from 10am to 2pm.

We'll have demonstrations of the usual stars of the Open Source world - OpenOffice.org, The GIMP graphics manipulation program, MythTV, StepMania, and a whole host of other useful applications.

If you'd like to see a particular free software project featured, want to demo your own favourite FOSS application, or have an hour or two to spare just to meet and greet and spread the message, please contact us. Or just come along on the day and see what the world of Free and Open Source Software has to offer.

Cheers,

Leah.
