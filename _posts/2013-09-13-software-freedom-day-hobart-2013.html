Software Freedom Day</a> is a global, grassroots effort to educate the public about the virtues and availability of Free and Open Source Software. Local teams from all over the world are organising events on 21 September.

Our Hobart SFD wiki page is here</a>. The Launceston page is over there</a>.

For this year's Software Freedom Day, our Hobart based team is showcasing some of the finest Free and Open Source Software that we can find. Specifically we'll be providing demonstrations on security your privacy online with Free Software and running a Free Software development session.

"Software Freedom Day is an opportunity to explore, discover and collaborate"</i></b>

For advanced people it is our intention to make improvements to ownCloud</a>,the Free Software cloud solution.

For those of us with an interest productivity, we're running introductions to writing python and javascript macros in LibreOffice</a>.

We will also be demonstrating several Free Software distributions that demonstrate software from the categories:

  • Media</li>
  • Home/Business</li>
  • Productivity</li>
  • Games</li>
  • Operating Systems</li></ul>
    The spirit of Free Software is community, and we feel that Software Freedom Day represents a way of building a stronger community both globally and locally.

    Location:</b> Chapel Room, New Sydney Hotel, 87 Bathurst St, Hobart (use the side entrance prior to 12:00PM).

    Google Maps Link</a> (http://goo.gl/maps/Ht5bE</a></a></a></a></a>)
