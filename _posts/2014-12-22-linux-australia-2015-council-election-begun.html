Those who have nominated for positions on the council do a lot of hard work to promote FOSS and Linux in Australia, as well as organising premiere conferences such as LCA and PyCon-AU. It is great to see there are several Tasmanian nominations standing for a spot on the council such as Joshua Hesketh, Chris Neugerbauer and recent Tassie ex-pat Craige McWhirter.

TasLUG operates as a subcommittee of Linux Australia. Right now, voting has begun to form the 2015 Linux Australia Council. Membership to Linux Australia is also free, and I would like to encourage all those who turn up to TasLUG meetings to also become a member of Linux Australia.

Head over to https://www.linux.org.au/membership/index.php?page=view-election&id=21 </a>and add your vote.
