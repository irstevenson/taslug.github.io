After sleeping off a weekend of lost sleep, lost flyers, and lost voices, I've finally made it here to report another great SFD event. Hobart weather managed to hold out despite forecasts to the contrary, and brought 60-70 people through the doors to learn more about the many different faces of FOSS software.

It was heartening to see how many of our attendees already had a good working knowledge of the software available; the growing awareness and enthusiasm about great applications such as OpenOffice, the Mozilla suite, and the GIMP was fantastic to see.

Many thanks to Joel for helping us promote our event on the ABC, and to Ben and Michael for doing an amazing job under pressure - and of course to everyone who dropped in to help us celebrate those wonderful people who produce the software that we know and love so well.
