The Hobart June meeting is on this Thursday 7:30pm Glenorchy Library (room 1). We'll be continuing our basic networking series with a demo of some of the graphical configuration tools for setting up SAMBA (and NFS).

Undoubtedly there'll also be discussion about ideas for software freedom day and the like as well - Remember if anyone has any ideas or wants to volunteer to help out please email me.

Considering a certain ruling in the US supreme court today there is no better time than to be promoting free software and raising the public's awareness about F/LOSS.
