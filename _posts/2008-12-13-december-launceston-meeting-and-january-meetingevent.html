January</strong>

As I'm sure you are all aware, Hobart won the bid to host next year's linux.conf.au (LCA), an international conference about open source software that draws approximately 800 people from around the globe. Though entry to the main conference requires a registration fee, there is an Open Day which will be open to the public and showcase some of the conference's content as well as general open source goodness.

The LCA Open Day will also take the place of January's Launceston meeting, and we are currently in the process of organising a bus from the north of the state to head down for the day.

If anybody would like to offer assistance with transport, please sing out. Questions regarding LCA can be sent to contact@marchsouth.org, whilst queries regarding the Open Day can be sent to openday@marchsouth.org
