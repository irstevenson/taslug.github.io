TasLUG will be celebrating Software Freedom Day on September the 10th 2005!
We are going to have a range of activities including giveaways, demonstrations, information and advice. We will also be showing the acclaimed documentary 'Revolution OS' - the story of how the Free/Libre Open Source movement began.

Details:
Demonstrations, etc. will be held at the Glenorchy Library Room 1 from 9:30 am until 4pm.
Revolution OS will be screened at 112 Liverpool St. at 7:00pm (film runs for 85mins).


Read on for further details:Demonstrations at the Library will include giveaways of the OpenCD - a cd of free software for the Microsoft Windows Operating System. We will also have GNU/Linux distributions for burning on-demand. We will be showing specific displays on:
Business productivity software
GNU/Linux based entertainment centre (Freevo)
Open Office.org
GNU/Linux on Low-end hardware.
Demonstations of the OpenCD for Microsoft Windows

TasLUG members will be on hand to answer any questions.
