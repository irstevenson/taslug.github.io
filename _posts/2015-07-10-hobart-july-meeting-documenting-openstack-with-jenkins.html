7:00pm - Documenting OpenStack with Jenkins - by Julius Roberts

"..Jenkins is a robot and the first word he learned was No.  Followed

by No, No, No, and then No.  Meeting Jenkins and taming him is a walk

through git (the preeminent distributed revision control system) and

Gerrit, a free, web-based team code collaboration tool.  Using these

things a team of developers can create and manage any distributed

codebase, including markdown for documentation, puppet manifests or

your kernel module.  And when Jenkins says "yes", magic things

happen.."
